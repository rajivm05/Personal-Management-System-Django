Personal Management System
For Educators

Personal Management System for Educators is a web-app tool designed to organize the essentials of everyday life. It can help you do everything from keeping a simple to-do list to managing schedules. No more thinking about unhandled pending issues, no more sticky notes on the desk, or missing out on important deadlines.

Simplicity, transparency, flexibility and expandability - designed for personal use.

All educators have various tasks, personal commitments, and deadlines to meet. In such a stressful environment, being organised is the key to efficiency and success. Our system offers a variety of tools designed with the following goals in mind:

  • Your central point for managing personal data.
  • Freedom of mind - keep control of schedules, notes, to-do lists all in one place.
  • Intuitive and user-friendly - designed to be clear and handy so that everything is organized and divided into the proper categories and subsections.

With this Management System, we hope to provide you all the support you need in managing your day-to-day activities. It will be our endeavour to keep improving your experience with this system as we go along.

To-Do List

By keeping such a list, you make sure that your tasks are all in one place so you don't forget anything important.

Schedules

Keep track of everyday schedules or any recurring things you have to do. You can avoid clashes by prioritizing tasks.

Issues

These include any ongoing/pending cases that have to be eventually resolved but it's not necessary to keep a constant eye on them.



Passwords

With the Passwords module, you can keep your passwords encrypted in the database, and look up the original password whenever required.

Bills & Payments

Keep track of recurring payments and see monthly summary of the expenses incurred on various categories of expenditure.
